History

A Movement is born.

MORE INSPIRATIONAL. LESS INSTITUTIONAL.

In 2005, Clutch was launched (as LG Consulting) by Tom Taft and Jeff Hibbert, founders of The Laurel Group, an executive search firm.

Through relationships with top executives they recognized demand in high-growth businesses for seasoned, focused professionals to deliver results.

The firm quietly grew to over 100 professionals serving diverse clients across finance, insurance, healthcare, technology, mobility and retail.

In 2013, Brad Myers became a partner, joined by technology executive Steven Anderson. Together, they focused on growing the company across the West Coast, precipitating the transformation to Clutch in 2014.
